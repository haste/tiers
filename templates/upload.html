Upload

This sadly doesn't work with the stock Android browser on most devices. Use Chrome/Firefox or some other browser for now.

How to

Android

1. Create a full agent profile screenshot
2. Wait patiently
3. Cancel share dialog with back button
4. Click Choose File above, locate the screenshot on your device and upload

iOS

1. Create a full agent profile screenshot
2. Wait patiently
3. Click Other
4. Click Save Image, then click Choose File above, locate the screenshot on your device and upload
